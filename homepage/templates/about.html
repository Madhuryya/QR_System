IITG Lab Equipment Scanner
An QR Management System


| About |


IITG_QR is a simple QR management software created for the requirements of a IIT Guwahati. The software is capable of keeping track of incoming and outgoing equipments with its all details. Each admin is provided with a profile where they can see the details of all laboratory equipments.


| Features |


  • Equipments List
  • Equipments details like current available equipments
  • Unique QR for unique instruments
  • Every equipments through the software is recorded
  • Separate profiles are maintained for users
